What I Do

Front-end Development

After taking Web Programming in college at Georgia State University, I fell in love with Front-End Web Development. This particular course covered HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I was able to get a good grasp on the foundational aspects of Web Programming and was able to further utilize and enhance my skills in my Software Engineering course. I specifically worked on Front-End projects such as the popular Game of Life, which allowed me to understand the fundamentals of JavaScript and how it is incorporated with HTML and CSS. I also was able to experience development utilizing React for allowing users to add and delete desired spotify songs on a Spotify Song Randomizer website created for my Software Engineering course. Overall, Front-End work is considered a strong suit of mine, and I would love to continue to learn more about it.
